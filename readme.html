‚ú¶ Why ClaritySuite

Modern software is noisy, networked, and constantly syncing. ClaritySuite moves in the opposite direction ‚Äî toward quiet and offline tools that respect your attention. As we move ahead to v1.0 from the current v0.1, hopefully we will also be able to add trustworthy to that list of adjectives. Each of our apps:

  • runs locally, offline
  • saves data only to your device (LocalStorage or filesystem)
  • has no telemetry, analytics, or background sync
  • strives for instant startup, minimal UI, minimal distraction

‚ú¶ Philosophy

  1. Local-first: tools should work offline and belong to you.
  2. Calm by design: software should create focus, not urgency.
  3. Transparency over tracking: no analytics, no sync, no advertisements.
  4. Beauty through restraint: clarity and silence are design features.
